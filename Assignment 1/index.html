Brooke's Fruit Pizza

An easy dessert that is a new experience every time with endless combinations.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter(softened).
  • 11⁄2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 21⁄4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 8oz Softened Cream Cheese
  • 1⁄2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 8oz Whipped Topping
  • A variety of your favorite fruits and/or berries

The Topping

  1. Put the softenned cream cheese, whipped topping and powdered suger in a bowl and stir until it is an even consistency.
    • It is fine to use a mixer.
  2. Place in the refrigerator while you prepare the crust.

The Pizza Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees;
  2. Grease a 14" to 16" pizza pan and lightly dust it with flour to avoid sticking.
  3. Combine the butter and sugar in a medium sized mixing bowl and stir until fluffy.
    • It is fine to use a mixer .
  4. Add the egg and vanilla to the butter and sugar mixture and stir thoroughly.
  5. Add the flour, salt, baking powder and stir until the mixture has a consistent texture.
  6. Spread the mixture onto your pizza pan.
  7. Place in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes.

Put It All Together

  1. Once the crust is out of the oven, let it cool.
  2. Spread the topping on the cooled crust.
  3. Place the fruit however you want and serve.
