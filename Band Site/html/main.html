30 Seconds to Mars

Formed in 1998 by Jared and Shannon Leto, 30 Seconds to Mars slowly gained popularity and has added Tomo Miličević to their roster. Their first studio album was released in August of 2002. They have since added 3 more albums to their discography with a 5th album expected in late 2017. Their songs have an often post apocolytic theme with lyrics that have dark undertones with uplifting and encouraging words for those that follow them. The fan devotion to the band has grown from endearing to an almost cult like following that the band openly accepts and encourages.
